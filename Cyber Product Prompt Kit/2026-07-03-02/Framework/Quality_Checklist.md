# Quality Checklist

Before finalising a product document, confirm:

- The 10-minute refresher is useful on its own.
- Product positioning is clear.
- Categories are identified.
- Problems solved are explicit.
- Architecture is explained.
- Integrations are practical and specific.
- Strengths and weaknesses are balanced.
- Competitors are included.
- Vendor claims are not accepted uncritically.
- References are included where available.
- Unavailable information is clearly marked.
